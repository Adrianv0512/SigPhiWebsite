Our History

Founded in 1924

The Delta Chapter of Sigma Phi Delta was established at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 1924, making us one of the oldest professional engineering fraternities on campus. For nearly a century, we have been dedicated to fostering excellence in engineering education and professional development.

Our chapter was founded during a time of rapid technological advancement, and we've maintained our commitment to innovation and progress throughout our history. Many of our alumni have gone on to become leaders in their respective engineering fields.

Historical Milestones

  • 1924 - Chapter founded at UIUC
  • 1946 - Post-war reactivation and growth
  • 1975 - Current chapter house established
  • 2000 - Centennial celebration of national organization
  • 2024 - Preparing for our 100th anniversary

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

We prioritize scholarship and intellectual growth, maintaining one of the highest average GPAs among engineering organizations on campus.

Professional Development

We prepare members for successful engineering careers through networking, workshops, and mentorship programs.

Brotherhood

We build lifelong bonds through shared experiences, mutual support, and commitment to each other's success.

Our Mission Statement

"To promote the advancement of the engineering profession through the development of its members into men of high ethical standards, to foster a spirit of cooperation and brotherhood, and to encourage the pursuit of knowledge and excellence in engineering education."

Our Chapter House

Home Away From Home

Our chapter house, located at 123 Greek Row Drive in Urbana, has been the heart of our brotherhood since 1975. The house provides a comfortable living environment for both active members and alumni visiting campus.

The facility features study rooms, common areas for social gatherings, a full kitchen, and accommodations for over 30 brothers. It serves as the central hub for all chapter activities and events.

House Features

  • Study rooms
  • Common areas
  • Full kitchen
  • Laundry facilities
  • High-speed internet
  • Parking available
