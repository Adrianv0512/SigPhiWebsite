About Our Chapter

Established in 1928

The Delta Chapter of Sigma Phi Delta at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign has been fostering excellence in engineering education and professional development for nearly a century.

As one of the oldest professional engineering fraternities on campus, we pride ourselves on creating a supportive community where engineering students can grow both personally and professionally.
