Membership Process

1

Attend Rush Events

Come to our events to meet brothers and learn about our organization

What to Expect

  • Casual, no-pressure environment
  • Free food and drinks at most events
  • Opportunity to ask questions about fraternity life
  • Meet brothers from various engineering disciplines
2

Interview Process

Formal interviews with our rush committee and executive board

Interview Details

  • One-on-one conversations with current members
  • Discussion of academic and career goals
  • Opportunity to express interest in leadership roles
  • Learn about time commitments and expectations
3

Receive Bid

Formal invitation to join our fraternity

After Receiving a Bid

  • Formal invitation extended within 48 hours of final interview
  • Attend Bid Day celebration to meet your new pledge class
  • Begin the new member education process
  • Officially become a brother after initiation ceremony
